What We Offer

Everything your hospital needs to manage washroom hygiene — from real-time monitoring to role-based management panels.

Our Services

Each module in SanitizeQ is built to solve a real operational challenge in hospital washroom management.

Real-Time Washroom Monitoring

Track every washroom across all hospital wings — live status updates for cleanliness, occupancy, and supply levels at a glance.

  • Live clean / dirty / supplies-low status badges
  • Occupancy tracking (Available / Occupied)
  • Location-based filtering by wing (North, East, South)
  • Summary stat cards — Total, Clean, Needs Attention

Complaint & Issue Ticketing

A full lifecycle ticket system — anyone can report issues and the system routes them through admin dispatch to resolution.

  • Public report form with location & issue type selectors
  • Automatic ticket creation with unique IDs & timestamps
  • Admin complaint management table with resolve actions
  • Support for cleaning, plumbing, supplies & maintenance issues

Staff Shift & Area Assignment

Admins assign cleaning personnel to specific hospital wings, ensuring full coverage and clear accountability.

  • Assign staff members to North, South, or East Wing
  • Staff view shows only their assigned washrooms
  • Team context switching (Janitor Team A/B, Maintenance Crew)
  • One-click "Mark as Cleaned" action for staff

Role-Based Dashboard System

Three distinct dashboard experiences — Admin, Supervisor, and Staff — each tailored with relevant data and permissions.

  • Admin: Full washroom, complaint, staff & inbox management
  • Supervisor: Task monitoring & complaint reassignment
  • Staff: Assigned areas, complaints & quick report form
  • Secure login with role validation & session control

Supervisor Operations & Oversight

Supervisors get a real-time view of cleaning progress and can reassign open complaints to the right team members.

  • Pending vs. completed cleaning stats at a glance
  • Staff task monitoring — status per assigned washroom
  • Complaint reassignment to any available responder
  • Active complaints counter for quick prioritization

Daily Reset & Scheduling

Start each day fresh — admins can reset all washroom statuses and clear resolved tickets with a single action.

  • One-click "Reset to New Day" from the admin panel
  • Resets all washrooms to dirty / needs-cleaning state
  • Clears resolved complaint tickets from the queue
  • Ensures a consistent daily operational workflow

Activity Logging & Audit Trail

Every action — cleaning, ticket resolution, staff assignment — is logged with timestamps to maintain full operational transparency.

  • Admin system activity log (all operations)
  • Staff personal activity history (my actions)
  • Timestamped entries for accountability
  • Scrollable log panels in admin & staff dashboards

Contact & Messaging Inbox

External users can submit inquiries through a contact form, and all messages are delivered directly to the admin's inbox panel.

  • Public contact form with name, email, subject & message
  • Messages appear in admin panel inbox table
  • MongoDB-backed persistent message storage
  • Supports integration requests, support & general inquiries

How It Works

A simple, efficient workflow from issue detection to resolution.

1

Issue Detected

A user, patient, or staff member spots a washroom issue and submits a report through the portal.

2

Admin Notified

The complaint ticket appears in the Admin Panel along with the washroom ID, issue type, and timestamp.

3

Staff Assigned

Admin or Supervisor assigns the relevant cleaning staff to handle the issue through their management panels.

4

Issue Resolved

Staff marks the task complete. The dashboard updates in real time and the ticket is closed in the system.
