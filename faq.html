Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about implementing SanitizeQ at your healthcare facility? Find answers below.

How does the occupancy sensor work?

Our occupancy sensors are non-intrusive infrared arrays mounted above the doorway. They passively detect when individuals enter or leave the washroom without recording any personal information, guaranteeing patient privacy while providing highly accurate usage data to the dashboard.

Do we need special hardware for staff?

No special hardware is required. The SanitizeQ Admin Panel runs on any modern web browser via desktop or tablet, and the mobile dispatch app can be installed directly onto staff's existing iOS or Android smartphones.

How can patients report issues?

Each washroom is equipped with a distinct QR code placard on the back of the door. When a patient or visitor scans the code with their smartphone camera, they are instantly redirected to the specific "Report Issue" page for that exact washroom. It takes fewer than 10 seconds to submit a report.

Is the data secure and HIPAA-compliant?

Yes. Our system never collects Protected Health Information (PHI). We solely process operational meta-data (door entries, staff resolution times, subjective feedback). All traffic is fully encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3 and stored utilizing AES-256 encryption.
